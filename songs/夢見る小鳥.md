# 夢見る小鳥 — original by Hayaya-P

referenced translation by Blacksaingrain. changed a buncha things up in maybe
good maybe bad ways

```
雪あかりほのかに　月の影を照らす        Snowlight faintly lights the moon's shadow
花びらが鮮やかに赤く色づく              Petals change to vivid red
舞い落ちるしずくが　水面揺らし消える    Falling drops ripple the water's surface and disappear
音の無い温もりが二人を包んで            Silent warmth wraps the two

明かり灯る 小径二人 歩いてたね          We walked a lit-up lane, didn't we?
ねぇ口ずさむ 唄が町に まぎれて揺れて    The song we sang to ourselves got lost in the town and swayed
坂に続く (石畳/凍える) 素足で           On the stone steps on the hill, we thought
どこまでも行けると信じていたよ          That our frozen feet could take us anywhere

囚われてた籠の小鳥が                    The little birds trapped in the cage
羽ばたく時は来るのでしょうか？          Will the time ever come to spread their wings?
あなたの涙受け止められるなら            If I can accept your tears,
もうこのままでもかまわない から         Then I guess we'll have to live on like this

闇に浮かぶ蛍が　炎のようにゆらめく      The fireflies that emerge in the dark flicker like a flame
霧と散るひとときを瞼に残して            This scene left on my eyelids fades dissolves like mist

影が映る 窓辺二人 笑ってたね            We laughed together by the window where we cast two shadows, didn't we?
ねぇ乱れた 髪 が風に 流れて 揺れて      Your scattered hair flows and sways in the wind
肩寄せ合い あたためる時間を             Standing side by side, warming time
いつまでも一緒に紡いでいたい            I want to be with you like this forever

心閉ざした過去のあなたを                You used to keep your heart shut
救える時は来るのでしょうか？            Will the time come for me to save you?
あなたの傷み分け合えられるなら          If you share your pain with me
もうこのままでも怖くない から           Then I won't be afraid to stay like this anymore

囚われてた籠の小鳥が                    The little birds trapped in the cage
夢見る時は来るのでしょうか？            Will the time ever come for them to dream?
あなたの涙受け止められるなら            If I can accept your tears,
もうこのままでも迷わない                Then I won't hesitate to stay like this
```
